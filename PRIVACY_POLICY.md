# Privacy Policy

**Ephemeris** is a free, open-source Android application licensed under the GNU Affero General Public License v3 (AGPL-3.0).

## Data Collection

Ephemeris does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app:

- Does not require an internet connection
- Does not request any device permissions
- Does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks
- Does not contain third-party SDKs that collect data
- Stores user preferences (theme, calculation settings) locally on the device using Android DataStore

## Third-Party Services

Ephemeris does not communicate with any external servers or third-party services.

## Astrological Calculations

All astrological calculations are performed locally on your device using the Swiss Ephemeris library. No calculation inputs (dates, times, locations) or results leave your device.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted in the project's GitHub repository.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
